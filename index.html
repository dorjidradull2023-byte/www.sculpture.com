Background History

The Aesthetics curriculum in the Bhutan Baccalaureate nurtures an appreciation of interconnectedness and nature. It builds an aesthetic mindset through diverse art forms to support a just and harmonious society.

The Aesthetics curriculum in the Bhutan Baccalaureate uses interdisciplinary learning, project-based activities, community engagement, and cultural exploration for experiential and reflective education.

Our Mission: To nurture an aesthetic mindset that fosters appreciation of nature and interconnectedness, inspiring learners to help build a just and harmonious society.
