Background History

The Aesthetics curriculum in the Bhutan Baccalaureate nurtures an appreciation of interconnectedness and nature.

The curriculum uses interdisciplinary learning, project-based activities, community engagement, and cultural exploration.

Our Mission: To nurture an aesthetic mindset that appreciates nature and interconnectedness.
